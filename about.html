TECHMELA

Aarohan at NIT Durgapur usually encompasses a wide range of activities across various domains such as robotics, coding, entrepreneurship, design, and more. The fest often features technical competitions like hackathons, robotics competitions, coding contests, and project exhibitions. Additionally, there are often workshops conducted by industry professionals, guest lectures, panel discussions, and cultural events. It serves as an excellent opportunity for students to gain practical experience, interact with industry leaders, and foster innovation and collaboration. For specific details about the latest edition of Aarohan, including dates, themes, and events, I would recommend checking the official website or social media channels of NIT Durgapur.

  

ESCAPE ROOM

The Escape Room activity at Aarohan, NIT Durgapur, is a highlight of the cultural-cum-technical fest, offering participants an immersive and challenging experience. Participants form teams and enter themed rooms where they must work together to decipher clues, solve puzzles, and unlock hidden compartments in order to escape within a specified time frame. With themes ranging from mystery to science fiction, the Escape Room activity not only tests participants' problem-solving skills and teamwork but also provides an engaging and interactive opportunity for attendees to enjoy during the festival.

  

CONJECTURE

Conjexture is a popular event typically hosted as a part of Aarohan, the annual techno-management fest at NIT Durgapur. It's a platform for students to showcase their innovative ideas and projects. Participants often present projects related to emerging technologies, such as artificial intelligence, Internet of Things (IoT), machine learning, and more. Conjexture provides an opportunity for students to demonstrate their technical skills, creativity, and problem-solving abilities. It also allows them to receive feedback from judges and industry experts, fostering learning and collaboration within the tech community. For specific details about Conjexture at Aarohan, such as rules, judging criteria, and past winners, it's best to refer to the official Aarohan website or contact the organizers directly.

  

ABHIVYAKTI

Abhivyakti is a cultural event often featured as part of Aarohan, the annual techno-management fest at NIT Durgapur. It provides a platform for students to showcase their talents in various cultural domains such as music, dance, drama, literature, and fine arts. Participants often compete in competitions like singing competitions, dance battles, drama performances, poetry recitations, and art exhibitions. Abhivyakti serves as a creative outlet for students to express themselves, explore their artistic interests, and celebrate cultural diversity. It adds a vibrant and dynamic dimension to the overall fest, attracting participants and spectators alike. For specific details about Abhivyakti at Aarohan, including event schedules, registration information, and past highlights, it's recommended to check the official Aarohan website or contact the organizing committee directly.
