CCA,RnD Cell

"Research is creating new knowledge."
- Neil Armstrong

During the Dark Ages, remarkable discoveries proliferated across diverse fields as humanity embraced a fervent pursuit of knowledge. The synergy between young minds and seasoned faculty propelled Research and Development (R&D) to flourish. To oversee and promote research initiatives, R&D Cells were established within institutes, coordinating and monitoring all research programs. These cells aimed to cultivate a vibrant research culture, particularly in emerging areas of Engineering, Technology, Science, and Humanities. Encouraging creativity among faculty and students was paramount, as it catalyzed fresh perspectives. Through lectures, demonstrations, paper presentations, and workshops led by both internal and external experts, students were inspired to explore and innovate.
